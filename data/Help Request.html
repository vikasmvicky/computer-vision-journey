This research provides a systematic review of explainable AI techniques used in healthcare applications. The study shows that explainable AI is essential in healthcare because clinicians must understand the reasoning behind predictions before using them in medical decisions. This study proposes a hybrid deep learning architecture for cardiovascular risk prediction.
